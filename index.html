  Attire Suggestions

Attire Suggestions?

Semi-Formal with Flair
We’ve had a few questions about attire, and if you’re anything like us, you might be curious about what other guests will be wearing—or you may just want an excuse to get a new suit or dress! Our ceremony and reception is indoors, and both the church and Grand Manse are air-conditioned. We want our guests to be comfortable but also to feel a bit special and have a chance to dress up! For the ladies, anything from a nice spring dress to something a bit more formal and cocktail-ish would be appropriate. (You might also want to bring a light sweater or shawl if you’re prone to getting a bit chilled, though once the dancing begins, you might not need it!) Pumps, flats, sandals, or anything in between are great shoe options, comfort being key. For the gentlemen, that might mean a dress shirt and slacks (sport coat and tie optional) or suit. Dress shoes, loafers, or similarly snazzy footwear would go well with your ensemble.
